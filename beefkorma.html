Beef Korma

Beef korma curry is a slow-cooked Indian beef curry that is known for its rich and creamy sauce, and tender pieces of beef. The Beef korma is unique for its rich, thick gravy made with blended cashews, coconut, yoghurt, caramelized onions and toasted spices.
